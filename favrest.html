My 5 Favorite Restaurants

Lang Van

Lang Van is located in the Plaza Midwood neighborhood of Charlotte, NC. They are a tried and true Charlottean favorite that has stood the test of time. My favorite menu item at Lang Van is the Pineapple Rice.

Jack Beagle's

Jackbeagles is located in the NoDa neighborhood of Charlotte, NC. My favorite menu item is the Jack Mac, a spicy garlic mac and cheese. While their mac and cheese may be my favorite their sandwiches are great as well.

My Kitchen

While it may not be a restaurant, I definitely enjoy cooking for myself in my own kitchen. I host a family dinner every Sunday, it's my favorite day because I get to share my food with others. Cooking is definitely not a chore for me.

Midwood Smokehouse

Midwood Smokehouse is located in the Plaza Midwood neighborhood of Charlotte, NC. My favorite menu item is their brisket burnt ends. You can't go wrong with good BBQ. Their classic Carolina bbq sauce reigns supreme.

Mert's

Mert's is located in Uptown Charlotte, NC. A good soulfood joint with tasty food and good vibes. Their soul rolls are one of the things I order every time I visit.
